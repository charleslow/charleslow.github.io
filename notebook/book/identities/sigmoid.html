Sigmoid Relationship to Bradley-Terry Model

Suppose we have scores si​,sj​ for objects i and j. Under the Bradley-Terry model, we can express the preference probability as follows:

P(i▹j)​=esi​+esj​esi​​=(esi​+esj​)/esi​1​=1+esj​/esi​1​=1+esj​−si​1​=1+e−(si​−sj​)1​=σ(si​−sj​)​

Where σ(x)=1/(1+e−x) is the sigmoid function.
